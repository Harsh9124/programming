WELCOME TO TECH FEST 2022

We are the Students of the Asha M. Tarsadia Institute of Computer Science and Engineering, and we are Delighted to Invite You in KHELA 2022 So in this KHELA 2022 there will be folowing Events to be Organised:

Code-Storm
This Event will be of three rounds and each round will be a elimination round and there will be one winner at the end (Note: c++/python will be the language )
  • In first round it will be syntax or logical based mcqs out of which best of 100 will be selected and in case of a tie the lowest submission time will be considerd
  • In the second round there will be the competritve programming will be there and the genral rules will be same and further instructions will be given at the time of event and here best of three will be selected
  • in last round a project will be given to finalists and they have complete the task in a fixed given timing it will be around 3-4 hours and accurate timmings will be given at the spot and the project will be judged by judges and winner will be decided
Ai-Gaming
In this Event the Participants have to pay 800 rupees to play the event and there will be an AR treasure hunt and Participants will be divided into 4 people squad randomly and they will play this game the squad which finds treasure first will be winner
Robotics
In this event the participants have take part in team of 5 people with a blueprint of a small working Robot and they have build it in a given time and the rest of the details will be soon updated on our portal
Logical Quiz
In this there will be a solo entry and the topic of the quiz is current affairs, reasoning etc and this will be an elimination round and in the final round there will be a buzzer quiz event and the winner will be decided from that
